What is iMemo?
iMemo is an android-based mobile learning tool application that is designed for the students of present generation. It can ease the student’s process of studying which can help them improve their knowledge on their lessons. The letter I in iMemo symbolizes one’s self. The word memo is an abbreviation for memorize which described as acquiring information, commit to memory, and learn by heart. It is also an abbreviation for memorandum that is originated from the Latin verbal expression memorandum est, which is the gerundive form of the verb memoro – to mention, call to mind, recount, relate, which means it must be remembered.
